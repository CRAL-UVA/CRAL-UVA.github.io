The Chandra Robot Δutonomy Lab, or CRΔL, is led by Professor Rohan Chandra. CRΔL solves autonomy and mobility problems with the goal of deploying robots in complex dynamic environments.

Dr. Chandra completed a postdoc at UT Austin from 2022 to 2024, advised by Dr. Joydeep Biswas and Dr. Peter Stone. Dr. Chandra obtained his M.S. and Ph.D. in 2018 and 2022 from the GAMMA Lab at the University of Maryland, College Park advised by Dr. Dinesh Manocha. His doctoral thesis focused on autonomous driving in dense, heterogeneous, and unstructured traffic environments.
